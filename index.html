Subject Choices After 10th Grade

Choosing the right stream after 10th grade is crucial for your career path. Explore the options and what they entail.

Science Stream

  • **Focus:** Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Math
  • **Career Paths:** Engineering, Medicine, Research, IT
  • **Key Skills:** Analytical thinking, problem-solving, logical reasoning

Commerce Stream

  • **Focus:** Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Math
  • **Career Paths:** Finance, Accounting, Business Management, Marketing
  • **Key Skills:** Financial literacy, organizational skills, critical thinking

Arts/Humanities Stream

  • **Focus:** History, Political Science, Geography, Psychology, Literature
  • **Career Paths:** Civil Services, Journalism, Law, Teaching, Design
  • **Key Skills:** Communication, creativity, critical analysis, research
