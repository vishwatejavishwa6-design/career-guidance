Your Academic & Career Roadmap (Class 10 & 12)

Navigate your journey from school to college with these clear steps.

1

Class 10 Completion

Focus on your core subjects and identify your strengths and interests. Explore different streams available after Class 10.

➜
2

Stream Choice (Class 11)

Choose a stream (Science, Commerce, Arts/Humanities) that aligns with your career aspirations and aptitude. Research future prospects for each stream.

➜
3

Class 12 Preparation & Entrance Exams

Dedicate yourself to Class 12 studies. Identify relevant entrance exams (e.g., JEE, NEET, CLAT) for your chosen career path and begin preparation.

➜
4

College & Course Selection

Research top colleges and courses based on your stream and entrance exam performance. Consider factors like faculty, infrastructure, placements, and fees.

➜
5

Admissions & Scholarships

Apply to your preferred colleges and courses. Explore various scholarship opportunities (government, private) to fund your education.
